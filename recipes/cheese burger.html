Cheese Burger Recipe

A cheeseburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked ground beef patty, typically topped with cheese, and served on a bun with various condiments and toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef (80/20 fat ratio is ideal) 100g per patty
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Burger Buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato Slices
  • Onion Slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Butter or oil

Steps to make:

  1. Make the patty: Mix ground beef with salt & pepper. Shape into round patties.
  2. Cook the patty: Heat pan → add little oil → cook patty 3-4 mins each side.
  3. Add cheese: Place cheese slice on hot patty → cover pan for 30 secs to melt.
  4. Toast the buns: Cut bun in half → toast with butter on pan till golden.
  5. Assemble burger: Bottom bun → mayo/ketchup → lettuce → tomato → patty with cheese → onions → top bun.
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy with fries or cold drink if you like 🍔🍹
