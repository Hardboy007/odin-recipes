Paneer Wrap Recipe

A paneer wrap is a popular Indian street food consisting of spiced and grilled or pan-fried paneer (Indian cottage cheese) wrapped in a soft tortilla, paratha, or chapati, often accompanied by various vegetables, chutneys, and sauces.

Steps to make:

  1. Cook paneer cubes with onion, capsicum, masala
  2. Take roti → spread mayo/sauce → add paneer mix
  3. Roll and toast on pan → ready to eat
