Masala Maggi Recipe

Maggi Masala is a popular Indian street food that involves cooking Maggi noodles with a blend of spices and mixed vegetables, creating a flavorful and hearty dish.

Steps to make:

  1. Heat oil → add chopped onion, tomato, green chili
  2. salt, red chili powder, turmeric
  3. water → boil → put Maggi + tastemaker
  4. for 2-3 mins → serve hot
